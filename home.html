Our Mission

The Princess Project promotes self-confidence and individual beauty by providing free prom dresses and accessories to high school teens who would otherwise be unable to attend their event. Our effort is made possible through invaluable volunteer, donor, and community support.
The Princess Project executes its mission through seven core values.

Our Values

In everything we do, we strive to honor these values so that we can continue to provide an exemplary experience for the teens we serve, volunteers that help, and our generous donors.
  1. Celebrate healthy body-image
  2. Value our earths resources
  3. Trust our community
  4. Offer meaningful experiences
  5. Include diverse representation of our community
  6. Create fun & respectful environment
  7. Include a diverse group of volunteers
